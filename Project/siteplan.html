Website Planning Document

Site Name & Logo

EcoTravel

Site Name: EcoTravel

Description: The name "EcoTravel" represents a travel service focused on eco-friendly, sustainable tourism options worldwide. It reflects the commitment to environmental awareness and sustainable travel choices.

Optional Domain Availability: ecotravel.com (subject to availability)

Site Purpose

The EcoTravel website provides travelers with information on sustainable travel destinations, eco-friendly accommodations, and tips for reducing environmental impact while exploring the world. The site offers booking services for eco-tours and educational content to promote responsible travel.

Scenarios

  • What eco-friendly hotels are available in Costa Rica that I can book through this site?
  • How can I learn about reducing my carbon footprint while traveling?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Dark Teal #005f73 - used for main headings, buttons, and links.

Secondary Color: Light Aqua #94d2bd - used for backgrounds, highlights, and accents.

Background Color: Light Grayish Blue #f0f4f8 - used for the page background for a clean, calm feel.

Typography

  • Georgia, serif: Used for all headings (h1, h2) to give a classic, trustworthy look.
  • Segoe UI, Tahoma, Geneva, Verdana, sans-serif: Used for all body text for readability and modern style.
  • Additional fonts may be added for special sections if needed, but the above two are the primary fonts.
