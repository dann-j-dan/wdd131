About Uganda

Uganda, known as the Pearl of Africa, is a land of diverse wildlife, lush landscapes, and warm hospitality.

From the snow-capped Rwenzori Mountains to the wildlife-rich Queen Elizabeth National Park, Uganda offers endless adventure.

Popular Activities

  • Gorilla Trekking in Bwindi
  • White-water rafting on the Nile
  • Hiking the Rwenzori Mountains
  • Exploring Kampala's nightlife
